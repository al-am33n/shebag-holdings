What We Do

Nigeria's Technology Partner

Shebag's Computing & Telecommunications Division has been equipping Nigerian institutions with world-class technology since the early 1990s. We supply, install, and support technology infrastructure for government agencies, educational institutions, oil & gas operators, research laboratories, and private enterprises.

We are an NITDA-registered ICT provider with global OEM partnerships, ensuring genuine equipment, full warranty coverage, and expert local technical support.
