Division 03

Engineering Division

Technical, scientific, and process engineering services — powering Nigeria's industrial and infrastructure backbone.

Our Services

Engineering Capabilities

From initial procurement to installation, commissioning, and maintenance — we deliver complete engineering solutions.

01

Scientific Equipment Supply

Supply and installation of scientific, laboratory, and analytical instruments for government agencies, universities, research institutions, and industries. We source globally, supply locally.

02

Process Engineering Support

Technical support for process plants in the oil, gas, and industrial sectors — covering process optimisation, troubleshooting, and performance improvement for existing facilities.

03

Water Engineering

Design, supply, and installation of water treatment and distribution systems. We serve municipal authorities, industrial facilities, and communities requiring reliable water infrastructure.

04

Rehabilitation & Maintenance

Technical and engineering support for the rehabilitation, maintenance, and construction of industrial and government facilities — restoring operational capacity efficiently.

05

Electrical & Energy Systems

Supply and installation of electrical systems, generators, and backup power solutions for industrial, commercial, and institutional clients across Nigeria.

06

Procurement & Supply Chain

End-to-end procurement management for engineering materials, spare parts, and specialised equipment — leveraging our global supplier relationships for competitive pricing and quality assurance.

Contracts & Procurement

Contract Services

Shebag Engineering has a strong track record in contract execution for government, parastatals, oil & gas companies, and international development organisations. We are experienced in all stages of the contract lifecycle.

Government & Institutional Contracts

Supply of scientific equipment, lab setups, and technical support to ministries, schools, and hospitals across Nigeria.

Oil & Gas Service Contracts

Engineering supplies and process support for oil field operations — the founding purpose of Shebag Holdings.

International Development Contracts

World Bank-funded contracts including our award under the Nigeria Federal Science & Technical Education programme.

Construction & Civil Works

Rehabilitation and construction contracts for industrial and institutional buildings, including water and electrical infrastructure.
