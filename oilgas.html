Oil & Gas Division

Shebag's founding sector — providing oil field services, supplies, and technical support across Nigeria's energy industry since 1988.

Founding Sector

Where the Shebag story began.

When Shebag Holdings was incorporated in 1988, the company was founded with a singular purpose: to indigenise oil field services and engineering supplies — sectors then dominated by foreign multinationals. For more than three decades, oil & gas has remained at the core of our business.

Today, Shebag is a NIPEX-registered vendor serving operators, EPC contractors, and regulators across Nigeria's upstream, midstream, and downstream value chains.

Our Services

Oil & Gas Capabilities

From engineering supplies to operational support — we deliver across the full value chain.

01

Oil Field Services

Onshore and offshore field support including well servicing equipment, drilling consumables, wellhead supplies, and field maintenance support for operators and EPC contractors.

02

Engineering & Equipment Supply

Supply of specialised engineering equipment, instrumentation, pumps, valves, fittings, gaskets, and other critical hardware sourced from globally certified manufacturers.

03

HSE & Safety Equipment

Personal protective equipment (PPE), gas detection systems, fire safety equipment, and other Health, Safety & Environment supplies critical to oil & gas operations.

04

Process & Maintenance Support

Plant maintenance contracts, shutdown support, process optimisation, troubleshooting, and rehabilitation of existing oil & gas processing facilities.

05

Procurement & Logistics

End-to-end procurement management for oil & gas operators — global sourcing, expediting, inspection, freight forwarding, and customs clearance into Nigeria.

06

Power & Electrical Systems

Supply and installation of generators, backup power systems, electrical infrastructure, and process electrical equipment for upstream and downstream facilities.
