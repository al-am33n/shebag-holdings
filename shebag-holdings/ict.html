ICT Division

What We Do

Nigeria's Technology Partner

Shebag's ICT Division has been equipping Nigerian institutions with world-class technology since the early 1990s. From computer hardware to telecommunications infrastructure, we supply, install, and support the technology that keeps Nigeria running.

We work with government agencies, educational institutions, research laboratories, and private enterprises — providing end-to-end technology solutions backed by expert local technical support.
