Agriculture Division

From Nigeria's fields to global markets — premium commodities, processed and exported with care.

National Leadership

Led by the President of FACAN

Shebag Holdings' Agriculture Division benefits from the direct leadership of Sheriff Balogun, President of the Federation of Agricultural Commodities Associations of Nigeria (FACAN) — the apex body representing all major commodity associations in Nigeria.

This positions Shebag at the heart of Nigeria's agricultural policy, regulatory dialogue, and international trade relations — giving our buyers and partners unmatched access, credibility, and market intelligence.

🌾

FACAN Membership Covers

All Major Nigerian Commodity Associations

From sesame and cashew to ginger, gum arabic, and cocoa — FACAN unites every key export commodity body in Nigeria.

What We Do

From Farm to Global Market

Shebag's Agriculture Division sources, processes, and exports premium Nigerian agricultural commodities. We work directly with farmers, cooperatives, and processing facilities to ensure the highest quality product reaches our international buyers.

01

Sourcing & Procurement

We work with verified farmers and cooperatives across Nigeria's key agricultural belts — ensuring traceability and consistent supply.

02

Processing & Quality Control

Commodities are cleaned, graded, and processed to international standards before packaging and shipment.

03

Packaging & Logistics

Proper packaging in jute, polypropylene, or buyer-specified containers, with full documentation (phytosanitary certificates, weight certificates, SGS inspection).

04

Export & Documentation

Full export documentation support — including NEPC registration, Form NXP, and customs clearance — for seamless international trade.

Agricultural Chemicals

In addition to exports, Shebag supplies agricultural chemicals and fertilizers to farmers and agribusinesses across Nigeria — supporting improved crop yields and food security.

Advisory Services

Agricultural Consulting

Drawing on Sheriff Balogun's leadership of FACAN and Shebag's three decades of commodity operations, our Agricultural Consulting practice advises governments, financial institutions, agribusinesses, and international buyers on Nigeria's agricultural value chains.

From policy advisory to commodity market intelligence, we bring sector insight that few firms in Nigeria can match.

📊 Market Intelligence

Pricing, supply, demand, and export trend analysis for buyers and policy makers.

📋 Policy Advisory

Strategic input for ministries, regulators, and development partners on agricultural policy.

🤝 Buyer Sourcing

Connecting international buyers with verified Nigerian commodity producers and exporters.

🌍 Trade Facilitation

Export documentation guidance, NEPC compliance, and trade route advisory for first-time exporters.
