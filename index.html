Who We Are

Nigeria's Trusted Diversified Conglomerate

Shebag Holdings Limited was established in 1988 with a clear mission: to indigenise critical service sectors dominated by foreign companies, with a particular focus on oil field services and engineering supplies in Nigeria.

Over three decades, we have expanded into a full-service conglomerate — competing internationally, creating employment, conserving foreign exchange, and building capacity for future generations.

Our Mission

Provide world-class services across every sector we operate in.

Our Vision

Export Nigerian expertise and commodities to global markets.

What We Do

Our Five Divisions

01

Computing & Telecommunications

Integrated technology solutions covering computer hardware, telecommunications infrastructure, physical security systems (CCTV, fire safety, access control), laboratory equipment, and IT training. Backed by global OEM partnerships with Dell, Cisco, Huawei, and HP.

Explore Computing & Telecom
02

Oil & Gas Division

Shebag's founding sector. We provide oil field services, engineering supplies, and technical support for upstream and downstream operations across Nigeria's energy industry. NIPEX-registered vendor with three decades of sector experience.

Explore Oil & Gas
03

Engineering Division

Technical and process engineering support for rehabilitation, maintenance, and construction. We supply scientific equipment, manage water engineering projects, and provide end-to-end support for industrial and institutional facilities.

Explore Engineering
04

Mining Division

Exploration and supply of Nigeria's rich solid mineral resources. Our mining operations are committed to sustainable extraction practices, regulatory compliance, and the development of mineral resource value chains for both domestic and export markets.

Explore Mining
05

Agriculture Division

From farm to global market — we source, process, and export premium Nigerian agricultural commodities including sesame, raw cashew nuts, dried ginger, garlic, gum arabic, and natural oils to buyers across North America, Europe, and Asia. We also provide advisory and consulting services drawing on Sheriff Balogun's leadership of FACAN.
